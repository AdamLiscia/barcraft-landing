FAQ

  • How do I sign in? Use Sign in with Apple or Sign in with Google — there's no password to remember.
  • Can I edit a classic cocktail? Yes. Editing a classic saves your own private version; the original library spec stays untouched, and you can reset anytime.
  • Why is the AI Mentor rate-limited? To keep the app fast and affordable for everyone.

Account & data deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all of your data at any time:

  • In the app, go to Settings → Delete account and confirm. This is immediate and cannot be undone.
  • It removes your specs, recipes, likes, favorites, photos, and AI Mentor conversations. Public cocktails others have already saved remain as their own independent copies.
  • Prefer email? Write to support@barcraft.bar from your account email and we'll delete it for you.
